Easy to Use

LearnDeck delivers a Beautiful designed User interface and amazingly smooth design flow that makes both students and teachers enjoy learning.

Course Management

LearnDeck provides a platform that helps you deliver course content easily to your learners. You can upload multiple content formats - PDFs, Slides, Videos, Audio, to create an engaging experience for your learners.

100% Responsive

No worries if all you have with you is a mobile phone or Tablet, Teacher can review submission on their phones anytime and anywhere. Mobility for us is very critical.

Learning Path

Combine multiple courses together to create a path of courses that leads your learners straight to their training goals. You can also allow students to complete targets individually or as a team.

Community Learning

A great training experience encourages your learners to share their learning experiences and knowledge gained from training. You can facilitate this communication between trainers and learners through LearnDeck

Inbuilt Assessment Tool

LearnDeck offers flexible testing and assessment options including multiple choice, text entry, short and long answer questions. Automatic and Manual Marking for online assessments are also supported.

Branding & Personalization

LearnDeck deploys the solution on a custom domain that reflects your brand. You can upload your logo, banner images, and choose your organization’s brand colors to create a more personalized platform.

Quick Setup

We can get you up and running within 24 hours. We provide seamless onboarding experience. We can also help you set up emails for your school if you do not have email accounts already.
